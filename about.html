IT Professionals Placing Professionals

Eiko Technology Solutions is a network of IT professionals. We have a core team of consultants who have worked together on a variety of projects over many years. We also support an extensive pool of prospective job candidates who are some of the most talented and dedicated people we know.

We started out as a software research and development company and have evolved our business to focus on the human side of tech, bringing great people together with great companies.

Not only does our job coaching really work, our consultants collective industry knowledge is an invaluable resource. We develop sophisticated techniques, using the hottest technologies in the world.

We take pride in our extended team of outstanding engineers, knowing that we’re playing a role in their success. What we love most about our work is the feeling that everybody wins.

Our clients remind us often how impressed they are with our developers' contributions and impeccable effort. We attribute this to the unique opportunity they have to grow with Lib Tech and the satisfaction they find in this partnership.

We continue to expand our services and expertise, recruiting new engineers every day and working to get them a job that’s a perfect fit. It’s not easy, but we try to get as close as possible.

Eiko Technology Solutions focuses on providing high caliber IT and business talent to companies in the Bay Area.
